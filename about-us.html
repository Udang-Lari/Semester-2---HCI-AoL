The History of V Kellogg's



W.K. Kellogg founded our company on February 1906, marking the dawn of a new era in the world of cereals. His groundbreaking invention of corn flakes set the stage for a century-long journey of innovation, pushing the boundaries of what was possible and transforming the way people start their day. With a passion for nutrition and a commitment to quality, W.K. Kellogg's vision was to make healthy food accessible to everyone. Today, we continue to honor his legacy by staying true to our roots and pursuing new ideas that make a real difference in people's lives. From our iconic cereals to our latest creations, we remain dedicated to nourishing families and communities around the world.
